Two stage Implants

Dental two-stage implants are a procedure where the implant is first placed into the jawbone and allowed to integrate before a second surgery attaches the abutment and crown

Tooth Decay

Dental tooth decay is the process where acids produced by bacteria erode the enamel, leading to cavities. If untreated, it can progress to deeper layers of the tooth, causing pain, infection, and potential tooth loss.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a dental specialty focusing on diagnosing, preventing, and correcting misaligned teeth and jaws. It often involves using braces, aligners, and other appliances to achieve a straight, functional, and aesthetically pleasing smile.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a dental procedure that involves removing infected or damaged pulp from the inside of a tooth, cleaning and disinfecting the area, and then filling and sealing it. This process saves the tooth from extraction and alleviates pain while restoring its function.
